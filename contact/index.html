Frequently Asked Questions About Selling

How fast can you close on my house?

We can close in as little as 7 days, though most sales complete within 14 to 21 days. Since we buy houses with cash, there are no bank appraisals or mortgage approvals to wait for. We work on your timeline and can close whenever works best for you and your family.

Do I need to make repairs before selling?

No. We buy houses in any condition throughout Hampton Roads. Whether your property needs minor cosmetic updates or major structural repairs, we will make you a fair cash offer as-is. You do not need to spend money on renovations, cleaning, or staging before selling to us.

Are there any fees or commissions?

There are absolutely no fees, commissions, or hidden costs when you sell to us. Traditional real estate sales typically involve 5 to 6 percent in agent commissions plus closing costs, but we cover all closing costs ourselves. The cash offer we present is the amount you receive at closing.

What areas in Hampton Roads do you serve?

We buy houses throughout the entire Hampton Roads region including Virginia Beach, Norfolk, Chesapeake, Portsmouth, Hampton, Newport News, Suffolk, and surrounding areas. If your property is located anywhere in southeastern Virginia, we want to hear from you and can likely make an offer.

What types of properties do you buy?

We purchase all types of residential properties including single-family homes, townhouses, condos, duplexes, and multi-family properties. We also buy properties in challenging situations such as foreclosure, probate, divorce, or those with code violations and liens.

How do you determine your offer price?

Our cash offers are based on the current market value of your home, its condition, location, and comparable recent sales in your neighborhood. We use transparent pricing and will walk you through exactly how we arrived at our offer so you can make an informed decision about selling your property.
