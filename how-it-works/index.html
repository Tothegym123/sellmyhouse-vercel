How Tidewater Property Rentals LLC Works

A Simple 3-Step Process to Sell Your Home Fast

1

Contact Us

Getting started is easy. Simply fill out our quick form or give us a call. Tell us about your property, your situation, and what's important to you. There's no obligation and no pressure.

We understand that every homeowner has unique circumstances. Whether you're facing foreclosure, dealing with an inherited property, relocating, or just ready for a change, we're here to listen and help.

2

Get Your Cash Offer

After you contact us, our team evaluates your property carefully. We consider the current market conditions, the property's condition, location, and comparable sales in your area. Within 24 hours, we present you with a fair, all-cash offer with no hidden terms or surprise fees.

You're not obligated to accept our offer. Take time to review it, think it over, or discuss it with your family. We'll answer any questions you have about the offer process.

3

Close On Your Timeline

Once you accept our cash offer, you choose when you want to close. We can close in as little as 7 days or take 30, 60, or 90 days if that works better for your situation. We're flexible and work with your schedule.

Our team handles all the paperwork, inspections, and title work. At closing, you receive your cash payment in full. No contingencies, no financing delays, no surprises. You walk away with the funds and move forward with your life.
