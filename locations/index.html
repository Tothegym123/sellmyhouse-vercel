Hampton Roads Service Area FAQ

Which cities do you buy houses in?

Virginia Beach, Norfolk, Chesapeake, Suffolk, Portsmouth, Hampton, and Newport News, plus surrounding communities like Smithfield, Carrollton, Yorktown, Poquoson, and Williamsburg-area homes case-by-case.

Do you only buy houses inside Hampton Roads?

That's our specialty. We occasionally buy outside Hampton Roads if it's within Virginia and the deal makes sense, but every offer is calibrated to the local market we know best — the Hampton Roads 757.

How does service area affect your offer?

It doesn't reduce it. We pay fair-market-value-minus-repairs in every city we serve. The only thing that changes is the local-comp calibration: a Virginia Beach oceanfront comp set is different from a Suffolk rural-parcel comp set, and we use the right one for your address.

Where do closings happen?

At a Virginia title company or real-estate attorney's office of your choice — typically in or near your city. We have closing relationships across Hampton Roads and can recommend a title company if you don't already have one.
