Frequently Asked Questions About Selling in Chesapeake

Quick answers to the most common questions from Chesapeake homeowners we work with.

How fast can I sell my house in Chesapeake?

We make a written cash offer on your Chesapeake home within 24 hours of seeing it, and can close in as little as 7 days. If you need more time — for a school year, a move, a probate timeline — we close on whatever date works best for you.

Do I need to make repairs first?

No. We buy houses in Chesapeake in any condition: leaks, code violations, fire damage, dated finishes, hoarder situations, inherited properties, foreclosures — anything. You don't clean, fix, paint, or stage. Hand us the keys as-is.

Do you charge fees, commissions, or closing costs?

None. There are no listing fees, no agent commissions, and we cover standard closing costs. The cash offer you accept is the amount that wires to you at closing.

How do you decide what to offer?

We pull recent Chesapeake comparable sales, estimate the after-repair market value, subtract repair costs and a reasonable margin, and present a clear, transparent number. We'll walk you through how we got there. No pressure, no obligation — if it's not a fit, you walk away.

Are you a local Virginia company?

Yes. Tidewater Property Rentals LLC is a family-owned, Hampton-Roads-based cash home buyer. We've purchased homes across Chesapeake and every neighboring city, and every closing happens at a Virginia title company.
