How We Buy Houses in Norfolk: Cash Purchase Only

Tidewater is a direct cash home buyer in Norfolk. When we make an offer on your home, we purchase the property outright in a single cash transaction at closing. You receive the full agreed-upon amount at closing — typically in 7 to 14 days. There are no payment plans, no installment arrangements, no ownership transfers after closing, and no long-term financing from us to you. This is a one-time cash purchase, handled at a Virginia title company, with the deed transferring to us the day we close and the cash wiring directly to your account.

Many Norfolk homeowners contact us expecting a creative financing deal. We don't offer those. We pay cash, close fast, and move on. If you need a simple, clean sale of your Norfolk house with the full payment on closing day, that's exactly what we do.
