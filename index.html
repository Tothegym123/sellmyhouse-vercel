Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions Hampton Roads homeowners ask us most.

How fast can you close on my Hampton Roads house?

We make a written cash offer within 24 hours of seeing the property and can close in as little as 7 days. If you need more time — for a school year, a Navy PCS, a probate timeline — we close on whatever date works best for you.

Do I need to make repairs first?

No. We buy houses in any condition across Hampton Roads — leaks, code violations, fire damage, hoarder situations, inherited or foreclosed properties. You don't fix, clean, or stage anything. We handle everything after closing.

How do you decide what to offer?

We pull recent comparable sales in your specific Hampton Roads neighborhood, estimate the after-repair market value, subtract repair costs and a reasonable margin, and present a transparent number. We'll walk you through how we got there. No pressure, no obligation — if it's not a fit, you walk away.

Are there fees, commissions, or closing costs?

None. There are no listing fees, no agent commissions, and we cover standard closing costs. The cash offer you accept is the amount that wires to you at closing.

Will you buy a house with tenants in place?

Yes. We buy tenant-occupied properties across Hampton Roads, with leases in place or month-to-month. You don't need to evict anyone — we honor the existing lease or work with you on a transition timeline.

What about probate or inherited properties?

We work with executors, heirs, and estate attorneys across Virginia. We can close once the estate has authority to sell, often without waiting for full probate distribution. Read our guide to selling a Virginia probate house.

Are you a local Virginia company?

Yes. Tidewater Property Rentals LLC is a family-owned, Hampton-Roads-based cash home buyer. We've purchased over 100 homes across Virginia Beach, Norfolk, Chesapeake, Suffolk, Portsmouth, Hampton, and Newport News, and every closing happens at a Virginia title company.
